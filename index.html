About Me

Hello, my name is Kelsey Selvaggi. I am 25 years old and I'm from a town called Doylestown in the suburbs of Philadelphia. I have recently began the journey to becoming a software engineer but that has not always been my goal. In High School, I never really knew what I wanted to do as a career but I was interested in math and science, leading me to start college at the University of Delaware as a Chemistry major. Four years later, I graduated with a Bachelor's in Biochemistry and I began working as a lab tech in a microbiology lab called Charles River in 2019. During the pandemic, I really started to reconsider a lot of things about my life and what I wanted. During this time, I realized I was trans and eventually began the process of transitioning. I also realized that my real passion and interest was computers and that I wanted to learn how to build software. This interest started from my early love of video games and I would love to sometime in the future maybe get into game development but for now I'm just looking to start a career in software development. Anyway, that's about it, check out the other sections for info on my interests and whatnot.
